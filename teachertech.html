Teacher Tech

Elevate your teaching practice and expertise in AI and STEM education

Register Now

Teacher Tech focuses on empowering educators to harness the power of AI tools for teaching and becoming certified STEM trainers. By exploring various domains, teachers will learn to maximize the potential of AI to create engaging and meaningful educational experiences.

Our program offers a blend of hands-on workshops and online courses, tailored to meet the unique needs of educators at all levels. Teacher Tech provides an in-depth understanding of AI tools, their practical applications, and strategies to enhance STEM education in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Who can join?

By becoming a certified STEM trainer, teachers will expand their expertise and reach, enabling them to inspire students to become future innovators.

By participating in Teacher Tech, educators can access a supportive network of peers, additional resources, and ongoing opportunities to deepen their knowledge and skills in AI and STEM education.
