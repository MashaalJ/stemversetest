Edventurers

Empowering Young Minds Through Playful Learning

Register Now!

Edventurer sparks curiosity and a love of learning in children aged 5-12. Our program uses a playful approach, blending interactive activities with engaging narratives. Children embark on exciting quests, mastering key skills in core subjects while fostering critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving abilities. Edventurer empowers young learners to become confident explorers of knowledge.

Program Details

Classes and Schedule

  • 2-hour classes on the weekend
  • 8 classes per month
  • Each domain lasts for a month before moving on to the next

Who can join?

This program is designed for adventurers who want to explore their curiosity, creativity, and problem-solving abilities in a fun learning environment.
