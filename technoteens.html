Tech Teens

A journey of discovery and growth in the technology realm

Register Now!

ETechTeens is an engaging program designed for students aged 13 and above. Focusing on areas like electronics, robotics, AI, VR, and 3D modeling, students will learn about cutting-edge technologies and their real-life applications.

In TechTeens, students will gain hands-on experience by working on projects that encourage them to design, build, and experiment with electronic devices, intelligent robots, virtual environments, and augmented reality experiences. The program fosters the development of essential skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration. Students will master high-demand technical skills while exploring their potential as future tech innovators.

Program Details

Classes and Schedule

  • 2-hour classes on the weekend
  • 8 classes per month
  • Each domain lasts for a month before moving on to the next

Who can join?

This program is designed for anyone interested in an exciting environment for learning about the latest technologies and fostering curiosity, creativity, and enthusiasm. .
