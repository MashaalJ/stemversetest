Slate 2 Tech

Prepared for the challenges and opportunities of a digital world

Register Now!

Slate 2 Tech represents the evolution of education and learning through technology, taking learners on a journey from slates to computers and virtual reality. This versatile program aims to teach literacy skills and essential technology know-how for all age groups.

Slate 2 Tech offers a dynamic learning experience that grows with you, as it provides age-appropriate and relevant content. Whether you're learning to read and navigate a tablet, or creating your first digital artwork, Slate 2 Tech meets you where you are and helps you evolve.

Program Details

Classes and Schedule

  • Monthly workshops
  • Guest workshops by Industry Experts
  • Online and Offline Sessions available

Who can join?

This program is open to learners of all ages, helping them build essential digital literacy skills for modern life and work.
